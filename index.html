    WE Are TCMU

    Welcome, and thank you for your interest in The Twin Cities Musicians Union, Local 30-73 of the American Federation of Musicians of the United States and Canada.

    TCMU represents professional musicians in Minneapolis and St. Paul, Minnesota, and surrounding areas.

    LIVE MUSIC MARKETPLACE

    Looking for live music for an event? You’ve come to the right place. The musicians and ensembles on this page are all staffed by members of the Twin Cities Musicians Union, local 30-73 of the American Federation of Musicians. They all have top quality skill, and experience that is second to none.

    Posted below are some of our featured Union members. To explore a group further, contact them directly. If they have a web site, click the web site link to go directly to their site.

    Thank you for considering union entertainment for your event!

    Twin Cities Musicians Union players are busy people. Below are blog posts that we use to highlight performances, gigs, shows, and other activities that our members are involved with.

    Send Us Your Photos!

    We love to share pictures showing what our union members have been up to! If you’d like to appear in these pages, please send pictures of your memorable performances, educational events, or even just shots of musicians having fun doing whatever (legal activities only). Send to: dave.tcmu@comcast.net. Thanks!
